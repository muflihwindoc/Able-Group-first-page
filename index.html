    About ABLE GROUP W.L.L

    Fully integrated hardware & building materials

    ABLE GROUP W.L.L. emerged in the State of Qatar in 1996, a professional dealer engaged in import and export, wholesale and retail distribution of sanitary items, painting tools, hand tools, power tools, safety products, plumbing items, carpentry items, chemical items, and other hardware and building materials. With its immense dedication to the customer satisfaction, the company quickly established itself as the trusted choice for the customers across the Qatar market.

    Our 27 years of professional experience which enable us to secure and maintain long term relationship with 1000 of factories in the mainland of China, Taiwan, Korea, India, Pakistan, Malaysia, Turkey, USA, Germany, UK, Italy, Russia and Middle East. As a result, we are able to source products that meet the quality and price requirements of our esteemed clients.

    Vision

    Able Group WLL prides itself on its quality and performance. The sole vision of the company is to deliver products and services conforming to International standards and satisfying the requirements and the expectations of our customers in respect of quality, cost, performance, safety and reliability.

    Mission

    Foster team work by nurturing talent, enhancing leadership capability and acting with pace, pride and passion. The company aims to become the supplier of choice, deliver premium products and services, and create value for our customers.
